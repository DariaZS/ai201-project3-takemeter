## Label Taxonomy

### Labels

**practice** — post asks about or describes a specific meditation technique, retreat, daily ritual, or observable practice. The question or discussion is about *how to do* something.

**philosophy** — post engages with Buddhist doctrine, texts, or concepts. Focus is on *understanding what something means*, not how to practice it.

**personal** — post shares the author's own experience, struggle, breakthrough, or life situation. The author is the subject, not a teaching or technique.

**resource** — post primarily recommends or requests books, teachers, podcasts, apps, centers, or links. The value is the pointer, not the discussion itself.
